Moving to Toronto Guide
- Popular expat neighbourhoods: Yorkville, The Annex, Liberty Village, Leslieville, Queen West
- Average rent for 1-bedroom: CAD 2,200-2,800 in central areas
- Public transport: TTC subway, streetcars and buses, monthly pass ~CAD 156
- Social Insurance Number (SIN) required for employment
- OHIP health insurance coverage begins after 3-month waiting period
- Canadian bank account essential for salary direct deposit
- Grocery costs ~CAD 300-500 per month per person
- Popular international schools: Toronto French School, Branksome Hall, Upper Canada College
- Family-friendly areas: North York, Etobicoke, Scarborough, Mississauga
- Prepare for winter temperatures dropping to -20°C with wind chill
