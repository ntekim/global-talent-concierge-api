USA Work Visa Requirements
- Valid passport valid for 6 months beyond stay
- Completed DS-160 online application form
- Approved petition from USCIS (e.g., H-1B, L-1, O-1)
- Visa appointment confirmation letter
- Passport-sized photos meeting US visa specifications
- Employment offer letter and supporting documents
- Proof of educational qualifications and work experience
- Visa fee payment receipt
- Interview appointment at US embassy or consulate
- Ties to home country demonstrating intent to return
