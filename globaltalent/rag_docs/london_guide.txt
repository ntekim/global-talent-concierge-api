Moving to London Guide
- Popular expat neighbourhoods: South Kensington, Canary Wharf, Clapham, Islington, Shoreditch
- Average rent for 1-bedroom: £1,500-2,200 in central areas
- Public transport: Tube and bus network, Oyster card system, monthly zone 1-2 pass ~£150
- National Insurance Number required for employment
- Council Tax must be registered at your address
- Opening a UK bank account requires proof of address and passport
- Grocery costs ~£250-350 per month for a single person
- Popular international schools: American School in London, International School of London
- Family-friendly areas: Richmond, Wimbledon, Hampstead, Greenwich
- Register with a local GP (doctor) for healthcare access
