UK Skilled Worker Visa Requirements
- Valid passport
- Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) from your UK employer
- Proof of English language proficiency (B1 level or equivalent)
- Salary meeting the general threshold of £26,200 per year or going rate
- Job at RQF level 3 or above on the eligible occupations list
- Tuberculosis test results (if applicable)
- Bank statements showing sufficient personal savings
- Valid ATAS certificate (for certain roles)
- Criminal record certificate (if required)
- Biometric residence permit application
