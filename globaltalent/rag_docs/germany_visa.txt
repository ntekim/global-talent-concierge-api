Germany Work Visa Requirements
- Valid passport with at least 3 months validity beyond intended stay
- Completed visa application form
- Biometric passport photos
- Proof of employment contract or job offer from German employer
- University degree or professional qualification (translated to German)
- Health insurance coverage valid in Germany
- Proof of sufficient funds to support yourself
- Rental agreement or proof of accommodation in Germany
- Visa application fee payment receipt
- Residence permit application upon arrival
