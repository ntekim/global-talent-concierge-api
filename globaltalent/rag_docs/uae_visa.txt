UAE Work Permit Requirements
- Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
- Job offer from a UAE-based employer
- Entry permit or employment visa from Ministry of Human Resources
- Medical fitness certificate
- Emirates ID application
- Labour contract signed by both parties
- University degree attested by UAE authorities
- Passport-sized photographs
- Proof of accommodation in UAE
- Visa stamping fee payment
- Residency visa sticker in passport
