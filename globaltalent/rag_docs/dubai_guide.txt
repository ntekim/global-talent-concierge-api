Moving to Dubai Guide
- Popular expat neighbourhoods: Dubai Marina, JLT, Downtown Dubai, Palm Jumeirah, Arabian Ranches
- Average rent for 1-bedroom: AED 60,000-90,000 per year
- Public transport: Dubai Metro and buses, Nol card system
- No income tax — major benefit for expats
- Emirates ID mandatory for all residents
- Employment visa sponsored by employer
- Grocery costs ~AED 1,500-2,500 per month
- Cultural tip: dress modestly in public, no public displays of affection
- Popular international schools: Dubai American Academy, Jumeirah English Speaking School
- Family-friendly areas: Emirates Hills, The Springs, Jumeirah, Mirdif
