Moving to Berlin Guide
- Popular expat neighbourhoods: Kreuzberg, Neukölln, Prenzlauer Berg, Friedrichshain, Mitte
- Average rent for 1-bedroom: €800-1,200 in central areas
- Public transport: U-Bahn and S-Bahn network, monthly pass ~€86
- Must register address (Anmeldung) within 14 days of moving
- Health insurance mandatory — either public or private
- German bank account recommended for salary and bills
- Learning basic German helps with bureaucracy (Bürgeramt)
- Grocery costs ~€200-300 per month for a single person
- Popular international schools: Berlin International School, John F. Kennedy School
- Family-friendly areas: Prenzlauer Berg, Zehlendorf, Steglitz
