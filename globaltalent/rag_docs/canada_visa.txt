Canada Work Permit Requirements
- Valid passport
- Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) or LMIA-exempt offer
- Completed IMM 1295 application form
- Job offer letter from Canadian employer
- Proof of relevant work experience and qualifications
- Police clearance certificate
- Medical examination results
- Biometrics (fingerprints and photo)
- Proof of sufficient funds
- Digital photo as per specifications
- Application fee payment
